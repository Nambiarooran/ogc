Welcome to OGC Stack Winter School 2024


The Geospatial Information Science and Engineering (GISE) Hub – IIT Bombay organized a successful 9-day Winter School program on the Open Geospatial Consortium (OGC) stack at IIIT Hyderabad. The program was tailored for engineering professionals from government, non-government, industry and academia, and focused on enhancing skills in modern geospatial standards and technologies.
